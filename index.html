Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know how to swim?
Yes, basic swimming skills are required for all surf lessons. You should be comfortable swimming in open water and able to swim at least 50 meters without assistance.
What should I bring to my lesson?
We recommend bringing a swimsuit, towel, sunscreen, water, and a change of clothes. All surf equipment (board, wetsuit if needed) is provided as part of your lesson.
How long are the lessons?
Standard lessons are 2 hours long, which includes safety instruction, land practice, and water time. Private lessons can be customized in length based on your preferences.
Is there an age limit for lessons?
Our standard group lessons are appropriate for ages 8 and up. For younger children (5-7), we offer special kids' classes with a higher instructor-to-student ratio. There is no upper age limit - you're never too old to learn to surf!
